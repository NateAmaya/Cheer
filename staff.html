Coach Sarah has been the Head Cheerleading Coach since 2018. Prior to coaching at MU, she served as an assistant coach at Illinois State University for one season. Sarah is a Big Blue alum and cheered all four years from 2013-2017. Sarah coached the first ever Millikin Squad to qualify and compete at College Cheerleading Nationals finishing 6th in 2022 and 5th in the nation in 2023.

Coach Emily became an assistant coach after graduating in May of 2022. While at Millikin, she cheered all four years. She was a part of the first team that competed at Nationals in Daytona, Florida in 2022. Prior to coaching at Millikin, she has coached gymnastics as well as help coach other high school cheer teams in the area.

Coach Alexis became an assistant coach for the team after becoming a Big Blue Alum in May of 2022. While attending Millikin she cheered all four years and competed as part of the 2022 National competition team that placed 6th in the Nation. Alexis has been a coach and leader for several other athletic programs in and around the Decatur area since 2016.
